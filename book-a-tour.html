Please note that, due to the myriad uncertainties created by the COVID-19 pandemic, we have changed our booking and cancellation policies.

A deposit of USD50 per person, per trip will reserve for you the dates of your tour. The deposit is payable by credit card (VISA/Mastercard) or check. Upon receipt of your deposit, we will proceed to make the reservations for your tour. You should hear from us within 5 business days regarding the status of your reservations.

Full payment is due 31 days before the first day of the tour and is payable by credit card or personal check. If you are booking a tour within the 31 days, we will process the full tour prices at the time of the tour confirmation.
