Requesting a Tour

Simply put, you choose a destination you’d like to go to, tell us when you’d like to go, and we set up your tour. Then, you decide if you want to keep your tour private to your own group or open it to other travelers, thereby taking advantage of our tier-pricing discounts.

Whatever the case, you’re booking a trip that’s guaranteed to run, no matter the number of participants, so you don’t have to worry about your tour being canceled due to so-called “low participation rates.”
