The GIROSOLE Team

(how we do what we do)

2017 marks our nineteenth year of crafting high-quality, custom tours in Italy. From the start, we’ve focused on providing expertly guided, fully personalized tours in small groups at moderate prices. And as our glowing testimonials show, we’re doing that and more.

In order to facilitate the complex process of efficiently creating on-the-spot personalized itineraries, we have offices in both Italy and the U.S. From our U.S. office, we consult with clients to build their tours and handle all billing and payment functions. Meanwhile, in our Italian office we make all arrangements, manage the myriad details, and ensure that your tour operates without a hitch.

All our guides live year round in Italy, most of them native Italians. This means that our expertise is truly Italy: we bring you inside the culture of Italy while providing expert guidance during your entire holiday – before, during and after your tour.

This year, we continue our growth with new destinations and activities, as well as the addition of exciting new finds to our existing trips. So if you’re planning on coming over to Italy, do contact us, and we’ll go make some discoveries afoot together.
