Self-guided tours

Personalize your trip according to your own preferences

Book any self-guided walking holiday with GIROSOLE and you tell us exactly

  • when and where you want to go
  • how long you want to go for
  • what level of accommodation you prefer
  • how much activity and walking you'd like to do
  • and anything else that'll make your holiday thoroughly perfect

This flexibility extends to all our self-guided holidays. You can opt to do just a portion of the walk, or extend the suggested itineraries however you please. Whatever the case, we provide you with all the tools you need to help you put your spirit of adventure into action. For our self-guided tours in Tuscany and on the Amalfi Coast, we outfit you with GPS-enabled smart phones — in addition to the trail descriptions, maps and guides to the areas you’ll explore. Each day, you’ll discover a new town or locale, and find your luggage waiting for you, transferred while you walk. And help is always just a phone call away, using the mobile phone we provide at no extra cost to every group.

So browse our comprehensive selection of self-guided Italy walking tours below, then contact us, tell us what you’d like to do and we’ll help you create a wonderfully memorable walking holiday in Italy!
